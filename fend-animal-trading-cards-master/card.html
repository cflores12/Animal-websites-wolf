Wolf

In Roman mythologoy, Romulus and Remus were raised by wolves. Wolves are the largest members of the dog family.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus
  • Average Length: 3.4 - 5.2 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 5 - 6 years in the wild
  • Habitat: Forests and Mountainous Terrain

Mexican gray wolves can thrive in the desert and brush in the southwest.
